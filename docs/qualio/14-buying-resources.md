# Buying Resources

A critical component of your eQMS investment is presenting a clear, logical business case to your internal decision-makers.

Comparing and evaluating key features, functionality and system attributes with a scorecard is a great way to pinpoint the best eQMS platform for you – and to convince your colleagues of your conclusion.

We built a vendor scorecard spreadsheet you can use right now to start comparing vendors.
